PREFACE

What is your name? Does anyone truly have a "name"? The name you have is most likely a legal name assigned to you so that you can be recorded and distinguished from others.

The name you have is your legal name, the name of your physical body. It can change and be perceived differently. Your conciousness doesn't have a "name". You must name yourself and remember it since you'll most likely be the only one "calling" yourself by that name; since no one can "see" your conciousness.

I believe a living being can be compared to a computer, one with a running operating system.
The hardware... is your body, the operating system... is your brain, and the user it creates... is you, the conciousness.

I believe a living being can be divided into two essential parts. Without either, the being is not truly living.
These parts are - your physical body (body and brain) and your mind (conciousness). The "mind" may be the neurons in your brain; at least that's what I believe.
